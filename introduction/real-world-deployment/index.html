    Real-World Deployment

    The Subject Interface has been successfully implemented at large scale in production environments, demonstrating its capability for mission-critical transportation operations.

    Current Deployments

    Netherlands

    National-level traffic management coordination with comprehensive C-ITS Service Provider integration, supporting:

    • Integration with C-ITS Service Providers for enhanced service delivery
    • Support for national-level traffic management coordination
    • Cross-jurisdictional data sharing and coordination

    Flanders Region of Belgium

    Regional traffic system integration with cross-border compatibility, featuring:

    • Regional traffic management system integration
    • Cross-border compatibility with Netherlands implementations
    • Multi-jurisdictional support across regional boundaries

    Application Domains

    Domain Use Cases
    Traffic Management Signal optimization, incident response, transit priority
    Safety Applications Collision avoidance, vulnerable user protection, emergency preemption
    Mobility Services GLOSA, traffic information, route optimization
    Infrastructure Integration Smart city platforms, environmental monitoring, emergency coordination

    Communication Scenarios

    Roadside to Central Communication

    • Traffic Light Controllers: Real-time signal state and timing coordination
    • Access Barriers: Automated barrier control and status monitoring
    • Sensors: Environmental and traffic data collection
    • Variable Message Signs: Dynamic message display coordination
    • Central Management Systems: Centralized monitoring and control

    Central to Central Communication

    • C-ITS Service Provider Integration: Data exchange with commercial service providers
    • Inter-system Coordination: Communication between traffic management centers
    • Multi-jurisdictional Support: Cross-regional data sharing and coordination

    Deployment Scale Support

    The interface supports implementations across various scales:

    Small-scale Pilots

    • Limited deployments and testing scenarios
    • Proof-of-concept implementations
    • Development and validation environments

    City-wide Systems

    • Urban traffic management integration
    • Municipal infrastructure coordination
    • Local service provider connectivity

    Regional Networks

    • Large-scale multi-city coordination
    • Cross-jurisdictional implementations
    • Regional traffic management centers

    National Implementations

    • Country-level C-ITS infrastructure
    • National service provider integration
    • Cross-border compatibility requirements

    Operational Applications

    Traffic Management

    • Real-time signal optimization based on traffic conditions
    • Incident management and emergency response coordination
    • Public transportation priority systems
    • Adaptive traffic control systems

    Safety Applications

    • Intersection safety warnings and collision avoidance
    • Vulnerable road user protection systems
    • Emergency vehicle preemption
    • Work zone safety coordination

    Mobility Services

    • Green Light Optimal Speed Advisory (GLOSA)
    • Traffic information services
    • Route optimization and navigation support
    • Dynamic routing based on real-time conditions

    Infrastructure Integration

    • Smart city platform connectivity
    • Environmental monitoring systems
    • Emergency services coordination
    • Multi-modal transportation integration

    Track Record

    The implementations demonstrate the interface's capability to:

    • Support mission-critical transportation infrastructure with high availability and reliability
    • Maintain flexibility for different deployment scenarios from pilots to national implementations
    • Support future expansion and new use cases through extensible architecture
    • Provide reliable communication at scale with proven performance in production environments
    • Enable cross-border interoperability between different jurisdictions and implementations
    • Facilitate integration with diverse stakeholders including public agencies and private service providers
